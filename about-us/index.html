GOAL & VISION

At Yard Deck, our goal is to help grow pickleball in India and create opportunities for players to compete at a higher level. We aim to build a strong competitive community where players can participate in tournaments, and gain match experience.

Our vision is to help Indian pickleball players reach higher levels by creating a professional and community-driven tournament platform focused on competition, consistency, and player development.

Through tournaments, rankings, live match experiences, and an active player community, Yard Deck is working to make competitive pickleball more accessible and exciting for players across India.
